About Virginia Nonprofits

A free, curated directory of nonprofit organizations across the Commonwealth — built for the people who want to find them and the organizations that deserve to be found.

What This Is

Virginia Nonprofits is a public directory. We catalog nonprofit organizations across the Commonwealth and make them easy to find — by region, by cause, by name.

This isn't a rating system, and it isn't a fundraising platform. We don't rank organizations or process donations. We do one thing: connect Virginians with the nonprofits working in their communities, so that the distance between "I want to help" and "here's how" is as short as possible.

The distance between "I want to help" and "here's how" should be as short as possible.

The directory is free to browse, free to be listed in, and open to any registered nonprofit doing legitimate work in Virginia. If an organization is missing, anyone can submit it for review.

Who It's For

Donors looking for trustworthy local organizations that match their values and geography — not just the national names that dominate search results.

Volunteers searching for meaningful ways to give their time close to home, whether that's a Saturday morning river cleanup or a weekly tutoring commitment.

Community members who've heard about an organization through word of mouth and want to learn more, or who simply want to know what nonprofits operate in their corner of the state.

The nonprofits themselves — especially smaller organizations that don't have marketing budgets or SEO teams. Being listed here means being visible to the Virginians most likely to care about their mission.

How Listings Work

Step 1

Submit an organization.

Anyone can recommend a nonprofit for the directory using the submission form. You don't need to work there — you just need to know they exist and believe they're doing good work.

Step 2

We review and publish.

Every submission is reviewed within 3-5 business days. We verify the organization is a registered nonprofit operating in Virginia, and then it goes live in the directory.

Step 3

Claim and keep it current.

Organizations can claim their listing to update their mission statement, website URL, and contact information anytime. A claimed listing stays accurate because the people closest to the work maintain it.

A Brenneis AI Project

Virginia Nonprofits is built and maintained by Brenneis AI, a web and automation agency serving Virginia nonprofits and small businesses. We built this directory because we work with nonprofits every day and saw how many excellent organizations remain invisible online. This is our way of closing that gap — a free public resource, no strings attached.

Questions?

If you have a question about the directory, a listing, or how to get involved, reach out at hello@virginianonprofits.org. We read every message.
